Coronavirus mortality estimates

Description

We study the impact of the COVID-19 pandemia by analysing the data from the John Hopkins catalogue. We produce dynamical and predictive models of the 'number of deaths' by calibrating the data to logistic curves using Bayesian parameter estimation tools. This interface is a rapid applet to produce predictions for countries with at least 300 fatalities. We will focus on the number of deaths as it is a more robust value; given that it is not as easily subject to the different rate of testing between countries. {{ content }}
